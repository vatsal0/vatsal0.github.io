Fearlessness

July 2, 2026

Back to writing

There is no such thing as a truly "fearless" person. Fear arises from uncertainty, and in any life uncertainty is inevitable. So if being fearless means to lack a sense of fear entirely, reaching such a state is impossible. It is possible, however, to experience fear but prevent it from influencing your thoughts or actions. In that case fear is neutralized; it exists but does not affect the outcome. Taking fearlessness literally, as in striving toward a life absent of fear entirely, is not the right metric. Experiencing uncertainty is necessary, otherwise you are living a completely predictable and mundane life. Instead, we probably want to be immune to fear. Don't try to not be scared, just do it scared.
