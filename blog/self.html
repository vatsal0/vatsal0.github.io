To My Younger Self

Back to home

You are going to fail many times, but only through those experiences will you learn to appreciate the glory of success.

Go out and embarrass yourself. You will find that through this, you actually become more confident.

Tell people you love them. Unrequited love may result in pain, but unexpressed love risks a lifetime of regret.

Do not let other people decide who you really are. This is a very subtle but dangerous trap. At the end of the day, you are in control. Never forget that.

You will get your heart broken. But do not let this scare you. Heartbreak means you had the opportunity to experience a love so strong that you did not want to let go, and for that you are immensely lucky.

Do the risky thing. You can always go back and correct course based on the mistakes you make. But you can never learn from the mistakes that you never made.
